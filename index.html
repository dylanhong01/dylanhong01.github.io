Value Proposition

* Pursues Opportunities: As an intern for the company PTC, I was initially asked to do some small-scale development work throughout the summer. However, I felt that the company was lacking in their ability to display virtual reality experiences, so I scheduled meetings and developed a new timeline for me to take on a larger project and develop technology that would allow for new shared VR experiences. After I completed my project, the department’s SVP asked me to stay on his team. I currently work there part-time during the school year.

* Self-Motivated: Because of my interest in developing and sharing innovative technology, 1.5 years ago I launched a consumer technology YouTube channel that accumulates ~40,000 views per month with a total of over 700,000 views. Self-taught in cinematography, video production, and media editing. This channel has allowed me to experience and share technology such as 3D printers, virtual reality systems, new ridable technologies, and more.

* Communicates: While on paper, I feel that my various technology-based projects have been exciting, a large reason for their success is the way I have been able to communicate them. Using my talents with video production and writing, I am able to document my projects in ways that allow them to be easily understood. This makes my work very easily sharable and consumable. I’ve often used this skill for the companies I’ve worked with to showcase their products more effectively.
