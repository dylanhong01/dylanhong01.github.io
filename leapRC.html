Hand-Tracked RC Helicopter

  • Purpose: Independant Research
  • Timespan: 2 Months
  • Software: Leap Motion, Arduino, Processing
  • Hardware: Leap Motion, Arduino, IR LEDs
  • Skills required: Java, C++, Simple Arduino Circuits

Project Info

Interested in exploring new technology, I came up with the idea of using the Leap Motion to control some type of hardware in the physical world. My final idea focused on using hand-position to send IR data to an RC helicopter. This project was one of my first steps toward exploring the interactions between people and exciting new technology.
